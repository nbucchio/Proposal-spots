Our standard

How we choose spots

Most places are beautiful. Very few are right for a proposal.

Every location on Proposal Spots is evaluated against five criteria: genuine seclusion at the moment that matters, quality of light at golden hour, ease of access without logistical stress, protection from interruption, and the emotional weight of the space itself. Some places simply feel different when you're standing in them — a quality that no photograph fully captures, and no algorithm can assess.

We research each destination in person or through trusted local partners who know the difference between a location that looks impressive and one that will make her cry. We look at crowd patterns by hour and season. We eliminate anything with unpredictable access. We reject spots that require a perfect Instagram caption to justify them.

The majority of spots we scout don't make it. The ones on this platform are here because we would propose at them ourselves — and we hold them to exactly that standard.
